# MacStream v0.7.0

MacStream v0.7.0 makes layout composition direct and makes presenter Cutout a
strict background-removal mode instead of a framed-camera approximation.

## Direct Canvas Editing

- Select the screen or webcam directly in the program preview.
- Drag a floating presenter or picture-in-picture camera to place it freely.
- Drag cropped content to pan it inside its frame.
- Pinch or use the canvas controls to resize and zoom while preserving the
  content beneath the gesture.
- Reset the selected source without resetting the rest of the scene.
- Share source dimensions and aspect-fill geometry between the editor,
  recording compositor, and RTMP output.

## Presenter Cutout

- Render only the segmented presenter over full-screen content, without a
  camera rectangle, border, rounded frame, or shadow.
- Pair every Vision person matte with the exact camera frame that produced it.
- Never expose the raw camera background when a matte is delayed, stale,
  mismatched, or unavailable.
- Use balanced Vision segmentation for cleaner edges while coalescing work away
  from capture and publishing callbacks.
- Limit matte updates to 8 FPS and bound the idle Cutout preview to 10 FPS at a
  320-pixel maximum display frame.

## Layout Inspector

- Organize the right-side controls into persistent Compose, Canvas, and Framing
  modes.
- Keep layout presets and presenter placement in Compose.
- Keep backgrounds, padding, gap, and radius in Canvas.
- Show precise controls for only the selected Screen or Webcam source in
  Framing.

## Performance And Validation

- Measured 1080p/60 idle Cutout preview CPU fell from about 50% to about 20% on
  the validation Mac without lowering encoded output quality.
- 412 default tests and 413 HaishinKit release-path tests pass.
- Default, HaishinKit, MLX, and combined HaishinKit plus MLX builds pass.
- The packaged app passed focused CuaDriver checks for direct canvas controls,
  Cutout output, and every nested Layout inspector mode.
- Publication remains gated on real RTMP ingest, Developer ID signing,
  notarization, stapling, Gatekeeper validation, checksums, and Sparkle update
  verification.

## Current Limits

MacStream remains a personal-use prototype. Validate important broadcasts with
a private or test destination first. Video editing, transcription, AI setup,
and model-controlled live behavior remain deferred.
